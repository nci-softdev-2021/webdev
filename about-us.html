Who We Are

Georgina's Kitchen is an online bakery where you can order a variety of handmade baked goods, from classic chocolate chip cookies to heavenly cakes. Not much of a sweet tooth? Try our new speciality: freshly-baked savory Italian bread.

Sweet or not, all of our products are made from wholesome ingredients, Irish butter, free range eggs, natural food colourings and baked in a professional environment.

Eggs and diary: Margaret Byrne’s farm in County Wicklow.

Chocolate: Cocoa 70% dark chocolate, Lactée Supérieure 38% milk chocolate and Blanc Satin 29% white chocolate from Cacao Barry.

Fruit: Keelings, Co. Dublin.

One more way we helping environment by using only compostable packaging.

While some of our recipes do not contain flour, our kitchens are not certified gluten free so there is always a risk of contamination.

We are always happy to provide vegan options of your cakes. Please, contact us to find out more.
