Who We Are

Georgina's Kitchen is an ethical Galway-based bakery specializing in guilt-free, handmade, heavenly cakes which you can order from the comfort of your own home! Feel like something different? From classic chocolate chip cookies to freshly-baked savory Italian bread, pop-in store to see our delicious daily specials!

Sweet or not, all of our products are made from wholesome, locally sourced ingredients, free from artifical colours and preservatives. We work closely with local producers to guarantee the highest standard of animal welfare. Animal products not your thing? Check out our vegan friendly options!

Free range eggs and dairy: Margaret Byrne’s farm in County Galway.

Fairtrade Chocolate: Cocoa 70% dark chocolate, Lactée Supérieure 38% milk chocolate and Blanc. Satin 29% white chocolate from Cacao Barry.

Fruit: Burke's fruit and veg, Co. Galway.

Worried about waste? We use only compostable packaging. Feed yourself and the planet!

While some of our recipes do not contain flour, our kitchens are not certified gluten free so there is always a risk of contamination.

We can cater to almost all dietry requirements. Please, contact us to find out more.
