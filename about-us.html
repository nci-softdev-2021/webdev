Who We Are

Georgina's Kitchen is an online bakery where you can order a variety of handmade baked goods, from classic chocolate chip cookies to heavenly cakes. Not much of a sweet tooth? Try our new speciality, for the time being in-store only: freshly-baked savoury Italian bread.

Sweet or not, all of our products are made from wholesome ingredients, locally sourced, Irish butter, free range eggs, natural food colourings and baked in a professional environment.

Eggs and diary: Margaret Byrne’s farm in County Galway.

Chocolate: Cocoa 70% dark chocolate, Lactée Supérieure 38% milk chocolate and Blanc Satin 29% white chocolate from Cacao Barry.

Fruit: Keelings, Co. Galway.

One more way we help environment is by using only compostable packaging.

We are always happy to provide vegan and gluten-free options of our cakes for you. Please, contact us to find out more.
