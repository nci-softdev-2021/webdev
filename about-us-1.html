Who We Are

Georgina's Kitchen is an online bakery where you can order a variety of handmade baked goods, from classic chocolate chip cookies to heavenly cakes. Not much of a sweet tooth? Try our new speciality: freshly-baked savoury Italian bread.

Sweet or not, all of our products are made from wholesome ingredients, Irish butter, free range eggs, natural food colourings and baked in a professional environment.
